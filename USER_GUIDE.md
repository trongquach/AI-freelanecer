# AI Freelance Marketplace - User Guide

Welcome to the **AI Freelance Marketplace**, the premier platform connecting businesses with top-tier AI experts. This guide will walk you through the core features of the platform, whether you are a **Client** looking to hire or an **Expert** offering your services.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Registration and Login
- Navigate to `http://3.27.209.83/`.
- Click on **Sign Up** in the top navigation bar.
- Choose your role:
  - **I want to hire an expert (Client)**: If you want to post jobs and hire freelancers.
  - **I am an AI Expert (Expert)**: If you want to offer your AI services and bid on jobs.
- Fill in your Full Name, Email, and Password, agree to the terms, and click **Create new account**.
- If you already have an account, click **Sign In** and enter your credentials.

---

## 2. Client Guide (Hiring AI Experts)

As a Client, your main goal is to find the right talent for your AI projects.

### 2.1 Posting a Job
1. Go to your **Dashboard**.
2. Click on the **Post a Job** button.
3. Fill in the job details:
   - **Title**: E.g., "Build a ChatGPT-powered Customer Support Bot".
   - **Description**: Detailed requirements for your project. (You can also click the **AI Enhance** button to let our AI automatically generate a professional description for you!)
   - **Budget Range**: Enter the minimum and maximum budget (in USD).
   - **Timeline**: Select your expected delivery timeframe.
   - **Skills**: Add relevant skills (e.g., Python, OpenAI API).
4. Click **Create Job**. Your job will be posted and visible to all experts.

### 2.2 Finding AI Services
Instead of posting a job, you can browse ready-made services offered by experts:
1. Navigate to the **Marketplace** from the top menu.
2. Browse through the available service cards or use the **Search** bar to find specific skills (e.g., "Chatbot", "NLP").
3. Click on a service to view its details, pricing, and the expert's profile.
4. Click **Order Now** (or **Contact Expert**) to initiate a contract.

### 2.3 Managing Contracts and Payments
- **Wallet**: Before starting a contract, you may need to deposit funds. Go to the **Wallet** page from the dropdown menu in the top right. Enter an amount and click **Deposit to wallet** (Funds are simulated in Sandbox mode).
- **Contracts**: View your active projects under the **Contracts** section in your dashboard. Here, you can message the expert and track progress.

---

## 3. Expert Guide (Offering AI Services)

As an Expert, your goal is to showcase your skills and win projects.

### 3.1 Creating a Service
1. Go to your **Expert Dashboard**.
2. Click on **Create Service**.
3. Fill in the service details:
   - **Title**: A catchy title for your service (e.g., "Custom AI Chatbot Development").
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation of what you provide. (Use the **AI Generate** button to automatically draft a professional service description based on your title!)
   - **Price**: Set your fixed price.
   - **Delivery Time**: Set the maximum delivery time in days.
   - **Tags**: Add relevant skills.
4. Click **Create Service**. 
   *(Note: Newly created services may require Admin approval before appearing on the public Marketplace).*

### 3.2 Finding Jobs and Submitting Proposals
1. Navigate to the **Find Jobs** section from the top menu.
2. Browse the list of open jobs posted by clients.
3. Click on a job to read the details.
4. Click **Submit Proposal** to apply for the job. You will need to enter your bid amount, proposed timeline, and a cover letter explaining why you are the best fit.

### 3.3 Managing Work
- Track your active projects, earnings, and proposals directly from your **Dashboard**.
- Ensure you communicate regularly with your clients to maintain a high rating!

---

## 4. Admin Guide (Platform Management)

Administrators have access to the **Admin Dashboard** to oversee the entire platform.
- **Service Moderation**: Approve or reject newly created services by experts before they go live on the marketplace.
- **User Management**: Monitor user activity, total transactions, and platform fees (10%).

---

## 5. Troubleshooting
- **Invalid Email or Password**: Ensure you are using the correct credentials. Passwords must be at least 8 characters long.
- **Errors during Job/Service Creation**: Ensure all required fields are filled and that budget/price values are greater than 0.

*Enjoy using the AI Freelance Marketplace!*
